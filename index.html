What?

Guide to the 21st Century is a 4-week online general-ed program with an 8 course curriculum, a book, and a community of learners.

Collectively covering the essential topics for life today, a series of self-analysis writting programs, and pedagogy that makes learning easy.

Why?

We believe every person must have a foundational knowledge base to succeed in life.

We thought school would provide us with one... it didn’t. Consequently, we decided to make one ourselves.

It turned out to be better than expected, so we decided to share it with the world.

Where?

It’s all online. You can do it anywhere.

When?

You can begin as soon as you enroll, or later -- you get lifetime access.

BUT, you must decide to start. If even 1% of you doesn’t want to do it, then don’t. Better to wait until you‘re ready.

How?

Watch the videos, answer the questions, trust the process, and see the world with new eyes.

If you’re really ambitious, you can go through the material in less than a week.

Who?

HERE'S WHAT WE COVER

Who are you?

A series of self-analysis writting programs

The Story of Everything

Considering the future always holds a strong resemblance to the past, it’s crucial to know where we’ve come from if we wish to get to where we want to go.

So, here we cover everything from the birth of the Universe to the happenings of the distant future.

The Game of Money

The course that never came.

Learn about the true nature of money, taxes, assets, liabilites, debt and all the other financial fundamentals that you cannot live without.

Money dictates our lives and it is deemed not worthy to be part of the education system?

Navigating the New World

Technology is intertwined with our lives and the bond will only strengthen as we move into the future.

Therefore, it’s critical to understand it such that you enhance yourself rather than be enslaved by it.

Here, we cover how to identify fake news, ripple-effects of digital posts, exponential technologies, and so on.

The Study of Thought

In a world rapidly evolving, your internal world - the mind - must be in a state of balance if becoming the victim is to be avoided; this helps in all facets of life.

We believe an understanding of the Science behind human emotions and the human mind is the solution. In other words, Emotional Intelegence and Psychology.

The Study of Knowledge

Information is everywhere, with it, complexity.

And It’s our inability to understand complexity that prevents us to make sense of life and make any kind of progress.

So, here we will cover logical fallacies, the different perspectives of life, the unanswered questions of the universe, and so on. In other words, the true essence of Philosophy.

Lessons from the Successful

Having a winner’s mindset is a key aspect in the journey to success - regardless of your definition of it.

This course will provide a whole host of insight into the mentalities, philosophies, principles, and other approaches to life that were a common thread between all the greats that have graced the surface of this planet.

How We Organize Ourselves

Politics defines the environment we live in.

It defines the rules within which we can pursue our hopes and dreams.

Need I say more?

Here, we definitely don’t give any political opinions, but provide a description of the political landscape, how it works, how it came to be, etc.
